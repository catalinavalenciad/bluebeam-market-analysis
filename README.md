# AEC Document Collaboration Software: Market Opportunity Analysis and Strategic Expansion Recommendations

## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end market opportunity analysis for Bluebeam, a leading provider of digital collaboration solutions for the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry. The analysis evaluates global and regional market sizing, competitive positioning, and EMEA expansion opportunities to produce data-backed strategic recommendations for Bluebeam's growth priorities over the 2026-2031 forecast period.

The project is structured as a professional market research deliverable, combining quantitative data analysis with strategic frameworks used in growth analytics and business development roles.

## Business Problem
Bluebeam holds a 4.6 G2 rating — matching Procore and exceeding Autodesk Construction Cloud — but has significantly lower market presence as measured by review volume (450 vs. 4,102–5,273 for competitors). This analysis investigates where and how Bluebeam should prioritize geographic and channel expansion to close this gap and capture a disproportionate share of the growing AEC software market.

## Methodology

### Section 1: Market Sizing (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Global, North American, European, and Middle East & Africa market projections (2026–2031)
- CAGR-based forecasting anchored to 2024–2025 reported industry data
- Bluebeam addressable TAM estimated from publicly available market share data

### Section 2: Competitive Landscape
- G2 aggregate review data collected March 2026 for Bluebeam Revu, Adobe Acrobat, Procore, and Autodesk Construction Cloud
- Competitive positioning matrix: G2 rating vs. market presence, bubble size representing AEC specificity
- Analysis of recurring positive and negative user themes across all four products

### Section 3: EMEA Market Opportunity
- Country-level analysis across Germany, United Kingdom, France, Netherlands, Sweden, Denmark, Norway, Saudi Arabia, and UAE
- Variables: market CAGR, BIM mandate status, existing Bluebeam presence
- Visualization colored by presence and opportunity level

### Section 4: Strategic Recommendations
- Composite scoring model weighting market growth (40%), market readiness (30%), and organizational readiness (30%)
- Three prioritized recommendations with competitive and organizational context

## Data Sources
- Fortune Business Insights (2025) — Global AEC software market sizing
- Grand View Research (2024) — End user segment breakdown
- Strategic Revenue Insights (2025) — Document management subset
- Statista (2024) — Europe construction and design software market
- MarketsandMarkets (2025) — Europe BIM market by country
- G2 (March 2026) — Aggregate product ratings and review themes
- Bluebeam press releases (2025–2026) — Office locations and expansion activity
- Nemetschek Group press releases (2025–2026) — Saudi Arabia partnerships

## Tech Stack
- **Language:** Python
- **Environment:** JupyterLab
- **Key Libraries:** pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn

## Key Findings
- The global AEC software market grows from USD 11.80B in 2026 to USD 18.75B by 2031 at 9.7% CAGR
- Bluebeam's addressable TAM grows from USD 0.47B to USD 0.75B over the same period
- Bluebeam's core challenge is distribution, not product quality — high rating, low market presence
- Three priority markets identified: Germany, Saudi Arabia, and France/Netherlands
